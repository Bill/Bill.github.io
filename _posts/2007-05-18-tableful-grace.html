I love reading about table-less layout with CSS. My experience though has been that it never really works for forms. I always end up resorting to tables to lay out my labels, controls and instructions. Well brother, Dan Benjamin and Dan Cederholm provide absolution.
Witness the tableful layout of the Cork'd signup form at the right. Be at peace. Use tables to lay out your forms. Life is full of sweet pleasures.
